# Security Policy

A guide on maintaining the integrity and security of our project.

## Introduction

This document outlines our security policies and delineates the procedures for reporting security vulnerabilities.

## Supported Versions

Currently, we only support the latest release of this project. Please refer to the latest release for the most up-to-date security measures.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

We appreciate your efforts in helping us maintain the security of our community by reporting potential vulnerabilities. Please adhere to the following guidelines to report vulnerabilities:

### Steps for Reporting

1. **Confidentiality**  
   Do not publicly disclose any details of the vulnerability until it has been fully addressed and a patch has been released. This precaution prevents potential exploitation and ensures the ongoing security of all users.

2. **Navigate to Security Advisories**  
   Visit our [Security Advisories page](https://github.com/ShohtaKouchi/general-template/security/advisories/) to start the reporting process.

3. **Create a New Security Advisory**  
   Initiate a new Security Advisory. Provide as much detail as possible about the vulnerability, including steps to reproduce it, if applicable. The more comprehensive your report, the more effectively we can address the issue.

### Our Commitment

Upon receiving your report, our security team will:

- Acknowledge receipt of your report within 72 hours.
- Provide an estimated timeline for the investigation and resolution.
- Work diligently to verify and remediate the issue.
- Keep you informed throughout the process.

## Questions

Should you have any inquiries or require further clarification, we encourage you to participate in our [Questions in discussions](https://github.com/ShohtaKouchi/general-template/discussions/categories/questions) section.

## Acknowledgement

Thank you for your support and cooperation. Together, we can maintain a secure and trustworthy environment.
